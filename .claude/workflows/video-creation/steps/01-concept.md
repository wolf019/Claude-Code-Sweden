# Step 1: Concept

Gather:
1. **Purpose** - What's this video for? (promo, demo, event, social media)
2. **Music Vibe** - What feel for the music? (energetic, calm, inspiring, fun, epic)
3. **Visual Style** - What look for the video? (retro/8-bit, modern/clean, minimalist, playful, corporate)
4. **Duration** - How long? (in seconds)
5. **Assets** - Any images/logos to include? (check if they specify a folder)
